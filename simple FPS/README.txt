The basic motion control and camera movement scripts are downloaded from the Unity assets store:
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/input-management/mini-first-person-controller-174710  

A simple FPS game where every time the player destroy a tree, the maze on the bridge will grow one more row until there are 15 rows in total.
In this project, I used some basic collision detection and implemented a perfect maze generation algorithm.
